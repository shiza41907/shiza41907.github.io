How are servers, routers, switches, work stations, and other hardware components are part of the internet?

Hardware component name Relationship between the hardware component and the internet
Servers Machines that provide service(s) to other machines on the Internet.
Routers On the internet, a device that connects several packet-switch networks or subnetworks
Switches Are essential to the internet in that they connect network segments which allow for effective and full communication, valuable network performance, and helps to connect devices for the purpose of sharing resources.
Work stations Computers that are connected to a local area network that in turn shares the resources of one or more large computers.
Modem A box that connects your home network to your Internet Service Provider (ISP)
Ethernet Cables Plugging into a variety of devices, ethernet cables connect devices to a local network

Describe the Internet Protocol Suite (TCP/IP) model of networking, in general terms, outlining how this protocol provides the data transfer mechanism required to establish client/server relationships.

When computers connect and transmit data between each other, they have to follow a set of rules to do so. These rules are made unviersally so that all computers can use them. These rules are called protocols. There are many different protocols made for different purposes. 'Internet Protocol Suite' is the term we use to refer to the protocols all together. The two most important protocols are Transmission Control Protocol (TCP) and the Internet Protocol (IP), which helps in making sure that data is delivered at the correct place without any errors.

What is hypertext?

Hypertext is a text that contains links to other texts, information or graphics. These texts can be typically accessed by the point-and-click method.

How is “the web” related to the internet?

"The web" is used on top of the infrastructure of the internet, in that "the web", also referred to as the World Wide Web, is a collection of information that is accessed via the internet.
